Toast Sandwich

Description

This recipe will walk you how to make Britain's finest culinary invention, the toast sandwich. Although the ingredients can be quite expensive, and the steps are quite intense, one is often amazed at the result. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • bread (3 slices)
  • butter (1 pat)
  • salt (optional)
  • pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. place 1 slice of bread in the toaster
  2. once toasted to perfection, apply butter and season with salt and pepper, if desired
  3. place toast in between two other slices of bread and enjoy
